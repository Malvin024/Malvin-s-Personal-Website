Series Reviews

I've written some of my all time favourites here! I hope that you can read it and see them by yourself!

Take it as my recommendations if you're bored!

Anime

Mirai Nikki / Future Diary

My rating: 10/10

Genre: Mystery, Horror, Thriller, Action, Psychological

Mirai nikki is probably my number one show out of all. This show is a very tense survival game based anime. This show is brilliant, because there are lots of twists and unexpected story going on! You don't even need that much time to complete the show. You can finish it in approximately 3 days? No matter what, give this show some love! Some of you may find it very disturbing, but It's really perfect for dystopian lovers!

Anime

Classroom of The Elite

My rating: 10/10

Genre: Psychological, School

Even though it's not like the Light Novel, I really did like it a lot. It's basically about students of an elite school competing so that they can be the "A" class. It's really captivating and I highly recommend watching the anime first rather than the light novel (so that you're not gonna be dissapointed). But overall, I really enjoy it a lot! It's gonna blow your mind, hence it shows how human acts in certain situation!

Anime

Konosuba!!

My rating: 10/10

Genre: Comedy, Parody, Adventure, Fantasy, Supernatural

There are many comedy based Anime out there. But only a few of them actually make me laugh so hard that I can't breathe (literally). But this show is a top tier comedy series. I'm sure most of you can probably relate to me if you watch this. The characters are very fun to watch because it's as if they are in a dialogue in their adventures.

Anime

Rokka no Yuusha

My rating: 10/10

Genre: Adventure, Action, Fantasy, Mystery

This is probably the best adventure based anime I've ever watched. The characters are well told and structured by the delivery. It really makes you want to have it for another season! It's been years though, and the second season is not yet to be released. Personally, I feel that it's really hard when you want to make the audience feel as if they are in the story. This show nailed it!

Anime

Fate Series

My rating: 10/10

Genre: Action, Fantasy, Supernatural

As you know, Ufotable studio created this piece of art. This is probably the anime with the best animation in the history of anime. From Fate/zero to the other series, all the components mix together really well. Especially about the fight scenes. The developments from the characters are so relatable and fantastic!

K-Drama

Twenty-Five Twenty-ONe

My rating: 10/10

Review:

This show without a doubt is one of the best series of all time, if not the best. I must praise all of the actors and team on making this masterpiece. This drama is about a memory of a fencer, it consists of friendship, dreams, and love. I had never known that a show can actually give off all kinds of emotions. The characters are just really unique and the plot is paced beautifully. Be ready to be sadden, it's a wonderful show. I don't want to give anymore spoiler for you !

K-Drama

Hometown Cha Cha Cha

My rating: 10/10

Review:

Sometimes, you want to chill and relax. Well, this drama is very healing and relaxing for you to watch. It wonderfully portrays a slice of life within a unique environment. It's about a dentist who moves to a village where she finds unusual people there, where she grows to love the chief of the village. Shin Min Ah (the actress) is very beautiful and is the best choice for this type of drama! There is also Kim Seonho with phenomenal acting!

K-Drama

My rating: 9.5/10

Review:
